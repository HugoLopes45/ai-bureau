## Update cadence
Most rates and thresholds refresh **every January 1st** (new tax year). URSSAF TNS rates can update mid-year; watch for changes. Usury rates update **quarterly** (first day of each quarter). The `last_updated` field inside each SKILL.md indicates when its rates were last checked here.
